How to Play

Goal

Score the most points by building expeditions (ascending card sequences).

Cards

5 colors. Each has numbers 2-10 plus three wager (x2) cards that multiply your score.

Your Turn

  • Step 1: Play or Discard — Tap a card in your hand. Then tap the matching color expedition below to play it, or the matching color discard pile to discard it.
  • Step 2: Draw — Tap the deck to draw, or tap any discard pile to take its top card.

Rules

  • Cards must be played in ascending order on expeditions.
  • Wager (x2) cards must be played before any number cards.
  • You cannot draw from the discard pile you just discarded to.

Scoring

  • Starting an expedition costs 20 points.
  • Score = (sum of numbers - 20) × (1 + wager count).
  • Bonus +20 if expedition has 8+ cards.

Menu
